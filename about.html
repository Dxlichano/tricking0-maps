About Dali

Hey, I'm Dali.

I started tricking after spending a lot of time at trampoline parks — just having fun flipping around. That’s where I found my love for movement. It wasn’t long before I wanted to push things further: learning more complex tricks, combining flips and kicks, and eventually getting into the wider world of tricking.

After an injury, I needed a new way to stay creative and connected to the scene. That’s when I started building **Tricking 0**, a game and community where people can share spots, maps, and videos — keeping that same energy alive, even when we can’t train in real life.

Dali Verse is just me putting it all together: the training, the game, the maps, and the people. It’s my little universe for everything I make and everyone who’s been part of this journey.

If you’re here, thanks for stopping by. Check out the maps, see the projects, or just look around — it’s all connected.
