Lore & Story

Axora: Discovered in 2012 by legendary tricker & cliff diver Axoria Fonso, the islands of Axora were once wild and untouched. In 2020, after a massive investment from Fullin Studios, Axora transformed into the paradise you see today — a hotspot where trickers and divers from around the world push the limits of movement against a backdrop of sun, sea, and cliffs.
