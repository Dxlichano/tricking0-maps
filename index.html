Lore & Story

Axora: Discovered in 2012 by legendary tricker and cliff diver Axoria Fonso, the islands of Axora were once wild and untouched. In 2020, after a major investment from Fullin Studios, Axora transformed into the paradise you see today — a global hotspot where trickers and divers push the limits of movement against a stunning backdrop of sun, sea, and cliffs.
